Committed to Safety

Our Drivers’ Safety Is Top Priority

In a rapidly changing world, safety remains our highest priority. More than profits and growth, the security of our drivers and all passengers is our main commitment. We have an operations staff monitoring every mile 24/7/365. The simple touch of a button and you can alert us or ask a question.

Our Riders Are the Reason We Drive

Slipping into the seat, we want our riders to be relaxed every mile. We take concerns and questions seriously and are available to address them any time of the day or night, all year long. Our operations team is riding right along with you.

Olawale Lasisi, Mevron CEO

Olawale Lasisi envisioned a ride-sharing program that would rival the current leaders in the industry and identified a need where rider and driver safety is of the utmost priority. “Getting people moving around the world while keeping them safe is a major responsibility. We strive to achieve a perfect record each and every day.”

Driving Forward
